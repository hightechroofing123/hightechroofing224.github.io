About Us

Welcome to A-1 Roofing - A Legacy of Excellence in South Georgia!

With a lineage of roofers spanning three generations, A-1 Roofing takes immense pride in our family's roofing legacy. Having served South Georgia with dedication and expertise, we are primarily located in Camden County, while actively undertaking numerous projects in the vibrant city of Savannah.

Our comprehensive range of roofing services caters to all your needs, ensuring your property remains protected and aesthetically appealing. Whether you require re-roofing, new roof installations, metal roof installs, tear-offs, or liquid applied waterproofing, we've got you covered.

As a licensed and insured family-owned business, we value the trust and accountability our clients place in us. Your satisfaction and the safety of your property are our top priorities. Throughout the years, we have built strong relationships with our customers, treating every project as if it were our own.

Beyond roofing, we extend our expertise to gutter repairs, siding installations, and repairs, ensuring your entire property remains in pristine condition. With additional services like gutter cleaning, roof cleaning, and pressure washing, we offer a seamless experience for your home or business.

Choose A-1 Roofing to continue our legacy of roofing excellence in the beautiful South Georgia region. Whether you reside in Camden County or Savannah, we're here to provide top-notch customer service, expert craftsmanship, and reliable roofing solutions. Contact us today to embark on a roofing journey tailored to your unique needs and the diverse landscape of South Georgia.
